About Our Municipality

Kalaignar Nagaram Municipality, established under the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920, serves a population of 2.5 lakhs. Our municipality is known for its heritage temples, educational institutions, and rapidly growing IT corridor. We are proud to be implementing the Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's comprehensive development scheme.
